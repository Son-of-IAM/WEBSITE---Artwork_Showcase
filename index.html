About

I bring the God dimension into the world through Art.

Son_of_IAM_ is a multidisciplinary creative, based in Lagos, Nigeria. His work primarily focuses on communicating the Christian Faith visually, using Faith symbols, themes and figures to communicate his Faith bodily as inspired by the Holy Spirit in Worship.

Promise (artist alias; Son_of_IAM), was born in Ogun State, Nigeria. Teaching himself how to draw as a child, evolving to learning Digital softwares like Photoshop, Blender 3D & Procreate in his early teenage years, to now using all these skills for a greater purpose; to serve the LORD.

Change has been a common theme in his Life and in his work that has explored various themes and approaches.

Origin

Born in Lagos, 2006
Looking back recently, I realized I was actually surrounded by few creatives, my mom was an event photographer. I can remember clearly how I used to play with her old analogue camera in the compound. By secondary school, I was drawing constantly, on my class tables, in my note books, and on A4 papers, drawing diverse figures just to practice.
2020 - Learning Photoshop
Years passed, we relocated and around 2020 my parent got me an old Pentium all-in-one desktop, a wired keyboard and a mouse. With those I installed Photoshop with the help of a new neighbor who was a graphic designer. He also helped with tutorial videos and textures on my drive. It wasn't a pivot — it was a new track in the same direction.
2022 - Learning 3D
In 2022 I took a big break from social media and started teaching myself Blender 3D. Studied the blender gurus — Polyfjord, Blender Guru, Ducky 3D — and fused their techniques with my vision. Back then, I just wanted to create and communicate a world only I could see in my head.

Common Questions

Do you take commissions?
Yes, but selectively. I take on a limited number of personal commissions each year. For the best chance of working together, reach out with a clear vision and timeline.
What's your typical turnaround?
It depends on the project. Simple pieces take 2-4 weeks. Complex campaigns can take up to 2-3 months. I'll give you a timeline once I understand your needs.
Do you sell prints?
Yes. We currently have only two offers for prints: Limited Premium Edition and Open Prints Offer. They are both available through the shop.
Can I license your work?
For commercial licensing (ads, products, media), please use the commercial Hub or email directly. All licensing is handled through Son_of_IAM_ studios.
What other products do you have your Art on?
I have my handcrafted art on diverse products from affordable to premium price range: stickers, wallpaper pack & prints. All available in Shop.
How does your Limited Premium prints and Open Edition prints work?
Limited Premium Prints: Available in limited numbers, and the offer is only open twice a year. This offer comes with: Certificate of Authenticity, larger sizes, museum grade printing, scriptural handcrafted scroll gift, Printed QR code to published article about purchased art, signature of artist (+edition numbers), possible special finish, collectors only wearables.

Open Edition Prints: Always available in the Shop, they come in small or standard frame size, canvas or regular art frame, artist signature (if requested), and Digital QR code to published article about purchased art.
