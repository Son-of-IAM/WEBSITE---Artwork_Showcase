About

I bring the God dimension into the world through Art.

Son_of_IAM_ is a multidisciplinary creative, based in Lagos, Nigeria. His work primarily focuses on communicating the Christian Faith visually, using Faith symbols, themes and figures to communicate his Faith bodily as inspired by the Holy Spirit in Worship.

Origin

Born in Lagos, 2006

Looking back recently, i realized I was actually surrounded by few creatives, my mom was an event photographer. I can remember clearly how I use to play with her old analogue camera in the compound.

By secondary school, I was drawing constantly...

Journey

From my school desk to the world

  • 2020 Learning Photoshop: My first exploration into digital art.
  • 2022 Learning 3D: I started learning blender 3D on YouTube.
  • 2022 NFT breakthrough: I found out about NFTs, I started and sold out collaboration collections.
  • 2023 First commission & Collaborations: 8+ international collaborations with artists across 5 continents.

Exhibitions
