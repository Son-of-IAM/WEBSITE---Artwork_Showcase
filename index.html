About

I bring the God dimension into the world through Art.

I'm Promise Adebayo a Nigerian Christian creative who uses his gifts in Service to God. My practice currently spans digital illustrations, blender 3D renders, and writing, all unified by a single goal; bringing the God dimension into the world and leading others closer to Him through Art imparted on me by the Holy Spirit in Worship.
