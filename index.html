About

Bringing the God dimension into the world through Art.

Promise Adebayo (artist Alias- SonofIAM) is a Nigerian creative who uses his gifts in Service to God. His practice currently spans digital illustrations, blender 3D renders, and writing, all unified by a single goal; bringing the God dimension into the world and leading others closer to Him through Art imparted to him by the Holy Spirit in Worship.
