About

I bring the God dimension into the world through Art.

Son_of_IAM_ is a multidisciplinary creative, based in Lagos, Nigeria. His work primarily focuses on communicating the Christian Faith visually, using Faith symbols, themes and figures to communicate his Faith bodily as inspired by the Holy Spirit in Worship.

Promise (artist alias; Son_of_IAM), was born in Ogun State, Nigeria. Teaching himself how to draw as a child, evolving to learning Digital softwares like Photoshop
