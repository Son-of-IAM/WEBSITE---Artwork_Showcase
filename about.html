About

Bringing the God dimension into the world through Art.

Promise Adebayo (artist Alias- SonofIAM) is a Nigerian creative who uses his gifts in Service to God. His practice currently spans digital illustrations, blender 3D renders, and writing, all unified by a single goal; bringing the God dimension into the world and leading others closer to Him through Art imparted to him by the Holy Spirit in Worship.

"This is eternal life: that they may know You, the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom You have sent."

John 17:3 — The ethos behind every work

Origin

Born in Lagos, 2006

Looking back recently, I realized I was surrounded by few creatives, my mom was an event photographer. I remember how I used to play with her analogue camera in the compound. By secondary school, I was drawing constantly, on my class tables, in my note books, and on A4 papers, drawing diverse figures just to practice.

Years passed, we relocated and around 2020 my parent got me an old Pentium all-in-one desktop, a wired keyboard and a mouse, with those I installed Photoshop with the help of a new neighbor who was a graphic designer. He also helped with tutorial videos, and textures on my drive. It wasn't a pivot — it was a new track in the same direction.

In 2022 I took a big break from social media and started teaching myself Blender 3D. Studied the blender gurus — polyfjord, blender guru, ducky 3D — and fused their techniques with my vision. Back then, I just wanted to create and communicate a world only I could see in my head.
