Common Questions

Before you reach out

Do you take commissions?

Yes, but selectively. I take on a limited number of personal commissions each year. For the best chance of working together, reach out with a clear vision and timeline.

What's your typical turnaround?

It depends on the project. Simple pieces take 2-4 weeks. Complex campaigns can take up to 2-3 months. I'll give you a timeline once I understand your needs.

Do you sell prints?

Yes. Our shop opens soon featuring Open Edition prints, Digital Wallpaper Packs, and more. Join the waiting list so you don’t miss out.
